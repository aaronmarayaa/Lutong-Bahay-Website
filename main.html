Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of food do you serve?

- We serve delicious lutong bahay, which means home-cooked meals in Filipino. Our menu features classic Filipino dishes made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.

Do you offer any dietary options?

- Yes! We understand that everyone has different dietary needs. We offer a variety of vegetarian and gluten-free options on our menu. If you have any other dietary restrictions, please feel free to contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

I got wrong food what shoud I do?

- We understand that mistakes can happen. If you receive an incorrect item in your order, please don't hesitate to contact us as soon as possible.

How long does delivery typically take?

- We pride ourselves on fast and fresh delivery. Our delivery times can vary depending on your location, but typically your lutong bahay favorites will arrive within minutes of your order.
