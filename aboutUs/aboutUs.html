Lutong Bahay: A Taste of Home
Lutong Bahay, which translates to "home cooking" in Tagalog, is more than just a restaurant - it's a family tradition on your plate. We bring the warmth and flavors of Filipino home cooking, using fresh ingredients and recipes passed down through generations.


Our Story:
Lutong Bahay started with a passion for sharing delicious food. Our roots are in the Pampanga region, known as the culinary capital of the Philippines. Here, our family honed their skills for generations, preparing classic dishes for town. In 1906, we brought this heritage to Nueva Ecija, Philippines. We started by catering events for friends and family, allowing them to experience the joy of Filipino home cooking. Fueled by their love for our food, we opened Lutong Bahay in 1957 to share these cherished recipes with the community.


Our Mission:

Authentic flavors
We use traditional recipes and techniques to create dishes that are bursting with Filipino flavors.

Fresh ingredients
We source the freshest seasonal ingredients to ensure the highest quality in every dish.

Warm hospitality
We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where you feel like part of the family.
